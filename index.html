Welcome to Frontiers in Physiology 2016

Frontiers in Physiology (FIP) is an annual research day for the Department of Physiology, providing students and faculty from across our four independent platforms and institutions with the opportunity to share their research.
This year's keynote speaker is Professor Tim Bliss, FRS renowned for his work on finding a neural mechanism for learning and memory.
